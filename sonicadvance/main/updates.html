Upload Date: 10/26/2025

Version 5.0.0 release:
Sonic Advance International has turned into Sonic Advance Revival!

The v5.0.0 of Sonic Advance Revival has been released alongside the Sonic Mobirev project to be part of the Revival series of Sonic Android apps.

This update introduces a brand-new options menu structure with options now categorized into different option pages!
Additionally, new options are also available such as the virtual pad settings!
