Game Builds

This is the page where you can download and test the numerous playable game builds.

The J2ME builds of Sonic Advance are initally released by Gameloft (except the Chinese version). Some of the builds are modded. To play a .jar game file, you need a J2ME emulator like J2ME Loader.

The N-Gage builds of Sonic Advance (also known as SonicN) can be found in the 176x208 section. To play N-Gage game files, you need an emulator like EKA2L1 and find a system ROM for N-Gage emulation.

Check out the different resolution sections down below, then check the different builds with their specifications.

All specifications indicating an issue are highlighted in yellow.
