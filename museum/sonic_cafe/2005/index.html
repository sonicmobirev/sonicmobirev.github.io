Sonic no Daifugou (ソニックの大富豪)

A Sonic themed Millionaire card game. The player controls Sonic, who is in the millionaire position against Tails, Knuckles and Amy. The game has the ability to upload high-scores to a server, and a statistics viewing system.

This game has been found and dumped as part of the Japanese phone preservation effort.

Sonic Jump

A Sonic jumping platformer game. Sonic jumps up on platforms to reach the top of the stage and defeat Dr. Eggman in each stage. This version of Sonic Jump contains 6 stages.

J2ME versions for release in South America and China have been dumped. However, the versions released in South Korea and North America (SEGA Mobile) are currently lost.

The Japanese version released for i-mode phones has been recently preserved and is playable via Keitai World Launcher.

Sonic the Hedgehog (2005)

A port of the Mega Drive Sonic the Hedgehog.

It was ported to North American, European, Chinese and Southeast Asian devices that supported J2ME and BREW, and those versions have been dumped. Depending on the platform, it was either a full game download or split into two parts. Low-end versions and demos have also been made. The Japanese releases on the FOMA devices are notable for being the only dumped versions to include sound effects and the Special Stages.

3 preinstalled Japanese versions from Panasonic phones have been dumped as part of the Japanese phone preservation effort. However, only the preinstalled base content is present, which includes the first three Zones (about half of the game). Additionally, entirely undumped releases for overseas i-mode phones are known to have existed as well under the title Sonic the Hedgehog: Contest Version, which may have included the sound effects and the Special Stages as well. Lastly, it appears that no proper dumps have been preserved of the South American J2ME localization from Tectoy Mobile.
