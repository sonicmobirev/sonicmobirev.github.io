Sonic Cafe (2003)

By late 2003, the Sonic Cafe website received an update.


Sonic Putter (ソニックパター)

A simple Sonic mini-golf game. This game features several gimmicks, such as panels that carry the ball around the course, warp panels, and breakable walls.

Neither the Japanese versions nor the J2ME conversions for Asia are dumped.

Platforms i-mode 504/503

Flicky

A mobile port of the 1984 arcade game. The aim of the game is to collect chicks who are scattered around each level, and return them to the doorway that Flicky used to enter each level.

Two versions exist, one for the lower-end i-mode 504 and the other for the higher-end FOMA devices. This game is also known to have been released in North America and Southeast Asia.

Date March 17th, 2003

Sonic Darts (2003)

A Sonic themed darts game. The player takes the role of Sonic and plays against Shadow.

Speed DX

A Sonic the Hedgehog Speed card game simulator. The player is Sonic and faces against other Sonic characters such as Tails, Knuckles and Amy. Being a remake of Speed, this game features improved graphics and a new Battle mode.

Sonic Cafe Page (Archived)
Date August 3rd, 2003
Platforms i-mode 505/504/503, FOMA 90X/70X, EZweb Brew 2.1/3.1
Preserved No

Sonic Reversi

A Sonic the Hedgehog Reversi simulation game. The player plays as Sonic and faces off against other Sonic characters such as Tails, Amy, and Chao. The player is given three Rings a day, with each match costing a Ring.

Neither the Japanese original nor the J2ME port for Asia are dumped.

Sonic Cafe Page (Archived) Gameplay Footage
Date October 27th, 2003
Platforms i-mode 506/505/504/503, FOMA 90X/70X
Preserved No

Tails no Flying Get (テイルスのフライングゲット)

A game to commemorate the release of Sonic Battle for Game Boy Advance. It features a mini-game from that game in which Tails has to collect 20 Rings as fast as possible while avoiding bombs. Hitting a bomb would lose 5 Rings.
