GAME LIST

Sonic Cafe (2004)

A version of Sonic Cafe for Vodafone Live! was launched on June 16th 2004.

Additionally, 2 games has been updated: Sonic Tennis and Sonic Darts.


Sonic Hopping (ソニックホッピング)

A Sonic jumping puzzle game. Sonic can take one hop backwards or two hops in any other direction. He has to get the Rings in each stage to pass while avoiding blocks.

This game was ported for J2ME devices for release in China and Southeast Asia, and those versions have been dumped. The J2ME release has some graphical differences, however.

Sonic Tennis (2004)

A Sonic the Hedgehog tennis game. The player plays as Sonic and faces off against other Sonic characters, including Tails, Knuckles, and Amy.

The 2001 version was replaced with this graphically enhanced version for newer phone models.

J2ME versions released in South America and China has been dumped, with minor graphical changes, such as with the power bar. Versions for overseas i-mode devices have also been known to exist.

The Japanese version has been found and is available in Keitai World Launcher.

Sonic Hopping 2 (ソニックホッピング 2)

Sequel to Sonic Hopping. Instead of a field, it takes place in a secret base.

Likewise, a J2ME port of this game for Southeast Asia is known to have been available, but neither that nor the Japanese version have been dumped.

Sonic Hearts

A Sonic themed Hearts card game simulator. The player plays as Sonic and competes against Tails, Knuckles, and Amy.

The game was later made available in the United States and Europe through SEGA Mobile.

Sonic Panel Puzzle

A Sonic themed tile puzzle game. In this game, the player plays as Dr. Eggman, who flies around on a tiled puzzle as the crosshair. The player must use him to unscramble a total of 9 puzzles of Sonic, Tails and more.

Sonic Gammon (ソニックギャモン)

A Sonic themed backgammon game. In this game, the player plays as Sonic in a game of backgammon against Dr. Eggman. Sonic plays as the white pieces, with Eggman then being the orange ones.

Sonic Darts (2004)

A Sonic the Hedgehog themed darts game. The player takes the role of Sonic and plays against Shadow.

This is the updated version of Sonic Darts with enhanced graphics. A downgraded port was released in Southeast Asia and has been dumped. It features much simpler graphics than the Sonic Cafe version.

This version was also available in the United States and Europe through SEGA Mobile, though slightly altered.
