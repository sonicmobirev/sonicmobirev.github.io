Sonic & SEGA All-Stars Racing

Introduction

Sonic and SEGA All-Stars Racing is a 3D racing game starring characters from Sonic and other SEGA properties. In this game, take control of a racer (each with their own race stats) and reach the finish line at the highest position possible. Along the race, you can gather rings for boosting and items to propel forward, defend yourself or attack opponents.

There are multiple game modes to choose from (Trophy, Arcade, Time Attack, Missions and Tutorial). In Trophy mode, there are 4 trophies to choose from, and each cup contains 3 race tracks. However, only the first cup is available from the beginning. Reach the highest position possible to win the trophy cup and unlock more trophies and racers.

Short versions

The short versions are available for mobile devices with screen resolutions of 128x128 and 128x160.

The short versions only contain 4 racers: Sonic, Amy, Tails and Eggman. Sonic is the only available racer from the beginning. No other racers outside of the Sonic franchise are present, despite Amigo and AiAi being shown on the title screen. Also, the racer stats are different from the other versions.

The tutorial mode is missing.

There are only 3 race tracks and 3 trophies.

TROPHY AND RACE LIST:

  • Diamond Trophy: Seaside Hill
  • Club Trophy: Seaside Hill and Blizzard Castle
  • Heart Trophy: Blizzard Castle and Casino Park

Medium versions

Unlike the other Sonic games, the medium versions are the most used version of the game and are available on any screen resolutions. Some versions with higher screen resolutions have touch controls for mobile devices with touchscreens.

The medium versions contain 6 racers: Sonic, Shadow or Amy (depending on the version), Tails, Eggman, AiAi and Amigo. In some versions, Eggman, Amigo and Tails are locked from the beginning.

There are 6 race tracks and 4 trophies. Depending on the version, either Detritus Desert or Tokyo-To is present.

TROPHY AND RACE LIST:

  • Diamond Trophy: Seaside Hill, Blizzard Castle and Casino Park
  • Club Trophy: Blizzard Castle, Casino Park and Curian's Mansion
  • Heart Trophy: Casino Park, Curian's Mansion and Detritus Desert or Tokyo-To
  • Spade Trophy: Curian's Mansion, Detritus Desert or Tokyo-To and Final Fortress

Long versions

The long versions are the definitive version of the game, but they are exclusive to mobile phones with a screen resolution of 240x320. Some versions also have additional graphical effects.

The long versions contain 10 racers: Sonic, Shadow, Amy, Amigo, Knuckles, Tails, Eggman, AiAi, Alex Kidd and Ryo Hazuki. The 4 last racers are locked from the beginning.

There are 8 race tracks and 4 trophies.

TROPHY AND RACE LIST:

  • Diamond Trophy: Seaside Hill, Blizzard Castle and Casino Park
  • Club Trophy: Curian's Mansion, Tokyo-To and Detritus Desert
  • Heart Trophy: Blizzard Castle, Tokyo-To and Final Fortress
  • Spade Trophy: Casino Park, Detritus Desert and Carnival Town

Android version

The Android port of the game was released somewhere in 2011. Information about its publisher is unkown, except that it was made in China. Its application package name is "com.pijiuping.GloftSORA".

This port uses the long version, but lacks any sound effects and the additional graphical effects.
