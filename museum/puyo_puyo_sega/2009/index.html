Puyo Puyo! Sega (2009)

In 2009, Puyo Puyo! Sega received the first brand-new Sonic games.


Sonic Dash Quiz (ソニックダッシュクイズ)

Sonic Dash Quiz combines elements of a generic quiz game with Sonic action.

In this game, the player controls Sonic and must compete against Tails, Knuckles and Shadow in various quizzes. The "dash" element of the quiz is that once the player has decided which is the correct answer to a given multiple choice question (out of 4). They must then run to the coloured Ring that matches the answer to the question on screen, dodging attacks from the other players and dealing some themselves if an opponent gets in the way. The player with the most correct Ring grabs at the end of the game wins.

Sonic Putter

A simple Sonic mini-golf game. Also known as "Sonic Putter Flash", this game is presumably an updated version of the original 2003 Sonic Café game Sonic Putter.

This game was released for the Sonic 18th birthday.

Sonic Bowling (ソニックボウリング)

A Sonic themed bowling simulation game.

In this game, Sonic, Tails, Knuckles and Amy are playable, each with their own playing style. As well as regular ten-pin play, there is also a Mission Mode with ten missions against Dr. Eggman. The player can upload their scores to the country-wide ranking system. Up to 4 players can play, with computers as the other three players if requested.
