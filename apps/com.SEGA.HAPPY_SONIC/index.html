App description

Brighten up the day and your Android device with Sonic The Hedgehog! Tap Sonic to discover fun sounds and cool animations in this Live Wallpaper exclusively for Android. The best part… You’ll never lose all your rings!

App history

Happy Sonic! Live Wallpaper is an Android-exclusive app published by Sega that adds a live animated wallpaper to the device. It was first released in Japan on July 5th 2013 through the Puyo Puyo! Sega subscription service. A few months later on December 20th 2013, the app was made available for purchase internationally through Google Play. In 2020, it was delisted from the store.

Activate the wallpaper

After installing the application, hold down the screen during the Android home menu and select the "wallpaper" option. Then, search for "Happy Sonic! Live Wallpaper".

Interact with the wallpaper

You can interact with the wallpaper by touching on the screen.

Touch Sonic's head to make him laugh and his body to tickle him. Touch the gift box to scatter rings and the HAPPY SONIC! logo to make it move around.
