App description

Help Sonic save the earth from Dr. Eggman’s evil plot!

As Sonic, leap as quickly as possible from platform to platform across seven unique levels of increasing difficulty, collecting rings and Chaos Emeralds, while defeating robotic enemies as you make your way to the top!

Dr. Eggman plans to destroy the Earth once again, and only Sonic can stop him! Are you UP to the challenge?

App history

The 2011 Android port of Sonic Jump is based on the AirPlay version.

This port that was only released in Japan and Chinese Mainland (Chinese version released on the eGame platform, currently lost media). It was originally released through the Puyo Puyo! Sega subscription service and as a free Android application from the Android Marketplace (now known as Google Play Store) on April 25th, 2011. Though the game required payment to unlock subsequent content.

Gameplay controls

The character jumps automatically anytime it lands on a platform. Tilt your device left or right to move the character and touch the screen to perform a double jump.

When the VIRTUAL PAD is turned ON, use the arrows to move the character left or right, and the "select icon" to perform a double jump. Exclusive to the Revival mod are 2 hidden buttons above the arrows. Touch above the arrows to move the character and perform a double jump at the same time.

Speedrun Mode (Revival mod only)

In Speedrun Mode, your goal is simple: complete the stages in the shortest time possible. You have unlimited number of lives, but you only have over 30 minutes to complete all stages.

Once you have completed all stages, your time record will be saved in the RECORDS menu.

Map Editor (Revival mod only)

In the Revival mod, you can use the Map Editor to create your own maps. Touch a block on the map grid to place, replace or remove an object.

On the top menu are numerous options. The MUSIC options are used to listen to music or select a music track for the custom maps, and the CONFIG menu is used to set configurations, such as the stage name, number of lives, sprites and tiles.

The SELECT ROOM option lets you change stages, but make sure to save your map before changing stages, otherwise, your edit will be lost.

You can load, save and play custom maps. The PLAY MAP leads you to the stage select menu. Make sure to select the dedicated stage to check the custom map. The PLAY MAP option only works after you saved the map.
