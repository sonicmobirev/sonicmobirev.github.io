Sonic the Hedgehog


Character Description


BIOGRAPHY:
Sonic is the world's fastest supersonic hedgehog! He's basically carefree, but he can't stand evil.

He is short-tempered, but if there's someone in trouble, he would go help them with his kind nature.

BRIEF GAMEPLAY DESCRIPTION:
Sonic is the main playable character of the game. His mechanics are simple.

STORY MODE

Sonic is present in all cutscenes. If you want to learn the entire game story, play as Sonic.


Abilities

Normal jump

Sonic has a normal jump height and falling speed.

Super Jump (Spin Attack)

Sonic's Super Jump is the Spin Attack.

This is a simple ability that allows him to perform a small double jump.

His Super Jump has a small jump height, but you can still make Sonic move left or right while using it.
